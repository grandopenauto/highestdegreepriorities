How billing works

Simple enough for a small business owner, protected enough for real-world AI voice usage.

What does setup cover?

Setup covers configuration, campaign scripting, dashboard/lead sheet setup, testing, and getting the first AI receptionist flow ready to launch.

Why usage credits?

Voice AI and phone minutes have real usage costs. Credits keep the system prepaid and transparent instead of bundling risky unlimited usage.

What happens when credits run low?

The account can be topped up with $50, $100, or $250 credits. Campaign usage can also be paused or limited to avoid surprise costs.
